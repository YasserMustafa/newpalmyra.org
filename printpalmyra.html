3D PRINT #NEWPALMYRA!

Celebrate and preserve the cultural heritage of Palmyra by 3D Printing the Arches of Triumph Globally on January 22nd

  1. Let us know you are pledging to help print the model by sending an email to INFO@NEWPALMYRA.ORG.
  2. Download the model of the Arch of Triumph.
  3. Print the model, any size. Be creative!
  4. On January 22nd, post photos of your model to Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter with the hashtags #ARCHOFTRIUMPH and #NEWPALMYRA.
  5. If you would like your model included in the exhibition, mail to:
    Taipei Contemporary Art Center
    c/o NEWPALMYRA
    No. 11, Lane 49, Baoan Street, Taipei, TAIWAN 103
    +886 2 2550 1231
